Kapha

Kapha dosha is associated with the elements of earth and water. It is responsible for the structure and lubrication of the body, as well as stability and strength. When Kapha is in balance, it promotes qualities such as calmness, patience, and stability. However, an excess of Kapha can lead to imbalances and various health issues.

Pitta

Pitta dosha governs metabolic and transformative processes in the body, including digestion, absorption of nutrients, and the body's temperature regulation. When Pitta is in balance, it contributes to qualities such as intelligence, focus, and a strong digestive system. However, an excess of Pitta can lead to imbalances and various health issues.

Vatta

Vata is associated with the elements of air and ether (space). It governs various physiological and psychological functions in the body, including movement, communication, creativity, and the nervous system.
